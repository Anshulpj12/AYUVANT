# Feature: Demand Alerts & Notifications

## Overview
The system notifies chemists about upcoming medicine demand, supply needs, and zone-level insights based on AI predictions.

---

## Alert Types

| Type | Color | Example |
|------|-------|---------|
| Critical | Red glow | "Amoxicillin shortage in Zone Z-4522 — Expected in 3 days" |
| Warning | Orange glow | "Paracetamol demand rising — Weekly increase of 40%" |
| Supply | Orange glow | "Fill supply: Cetirizine stock below 15% threshold" |
| Info | Cyan glow | "Zone Z-4518 demand stable — No action needed" |
| Seasonal | Cyan glow | "Antihistamines demand expected to rise next month" |

---

## What Gets Notified
- When and where upcoming demand will be
- Which medicine in which zone
- Tell chemist to fill supply of any medicine which is needed
- Shortage countdowns
- Seasonal trend warnings

---

## Alert Categories
- All
- Shortage
- Zone Demand
- Supply
- Trending

---

## Actions from Alerts
- "Order Now" — quick order for that medicine
- "View Details" — see full prediction data
